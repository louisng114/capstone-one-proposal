Capstone One Proposal

  1. CalculTube:

    For any high school calculus topic, users can nominate and vote on YouTube videos that they think best explains the topic. (Think of it like Reddit)

    • List of topics are manually added based on the AP calculus standards.
    • Can have different categories to vote on: best basics, best in depth, funniest, etc.

    API: https://developers.google.com/youtube/v3/getting-started

  2. OEIS Explorer:

    Similar as above but for OEIS (On-Line Encyclopedia for Integer Sequences https://oeis.org/).

    • Users can rate sequences for fun level and difficulty level to research.
    • Math teachers can use it to assign projects.
    • Math enthusiasts can use it to broaden their horizon.

    API: https://oeis.org/wiki/JSON_Format,_Compressed_Files

  3. Kan-nections

    Connections (https://www.nytimes.com/games/connections) clone but for Japanese Kanji.

    • Studying tool for learners of Japanese.
    • Fun for those who already knows Kanji
    • Users can select JLPT level.

    API: https://kanjiapi.dev/
